Introduction

In this document you will find the history of every weight class within the UFC. This document will not include champions from other organisations such as WEC, Bellator, or Pride.

UFC Flyweight Division

The UFC Flyweight Division was formed on the 22nd of September 2012, when Demetrious Johnson and Joseph Benavidez fought for the vacant title.

The list of champions is found below.

  1. Demetrious Johnson (Sept 2012-Aug 2018)
  2. Henry Cejudo (Aug 2018-Feb 2020)
  3. Deiveson Figueiredo (July 2020-June 2021)
  4. Brandon Moreno (June 2021-Jan 2022)
  5. Deiveson Figueiredo (Jan-2022-Present)

UFC Bantamweight Division

The UFC Bantamweight Division was formed on the 16th of December 2010 when Dominic Cruz and Scott Jorgensen fought for the vacant title.

The list of champions is found below.

  1. Dominic Cruz (Dec 2010-Jan 2014)
  2. Renan Barao (Jan 2014-May 2014)
  3. T.J Dillashaw (May 2014-Jan 2016)
  4. Dominic Cruz (Jan 2016-Jan 2016)
  5. Cody Garbrandt (Dec-2016-Nov 2017)
  6. T.J Dillashaw (Nov 2017-Mar 2019)
  7. Henry Cejudo (June 2019-May 2020)
  8. Petr Yan (July 2020-Mar 2021)
  9. Aljamain Sterling (Mar 2021-Present)

UFC Featherweight Division

The UFC Featherweight Division was formed on the 20th of November 2010 when Jose Aldo was awarded the title during the WEC/UFC merger, he was awarded the title due to sheer dominance in the WEC Featherweight Division, and nobody disputed it at the time.

The list of champions is found below.

  1. Jose Aldo (Nov 2010-July 2015)
  2. Conor Mcgregor (July 2015-Nov 2016)
  3. Jose Aldo (Nov 2016-June 2017)
  4. Max Holloway (June 2017-Dec 2019)
  5. Alexander Volkanovski (Dec 2019-Present)

  11. Charles Olivera (May 2021-Present)
